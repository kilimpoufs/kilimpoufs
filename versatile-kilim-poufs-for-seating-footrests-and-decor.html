  • Store in a cool, dry place during extreme weather conditions to prevent damage.

Cleaning Tips for Longevity

To maintain the beauty and longevity of your Versatile Kilim Poufs for Seating, Footrests, and Decor, regular cleaning is essential. It is advisable to gently vacuum your poufs, focusing on crevices where dust may accumulate. For stains, prompt action is key; blotting rather than rubbing can prevent further damage. If you have a red pouf or any square-shaped kilim ottomans, using a damp cloth with mild detergent can help restore their vibrant colors without compromising the fabric.

For outdoor settings or areas near coffee tables, consider using built-in trays to minimize spills and protect your poufs. Placing your versatile kilim poufs strategically can also reduce wear. Whether you have a round storage ottoman or other poufs that showcase bold patterns, keeping them away from direct sunlight will preserve their overall style and texture.
